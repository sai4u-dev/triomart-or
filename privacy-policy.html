Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15-09-2025 | Last Updated: 15-09-2025

At TrioMart, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile application, or services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address, payment details.
  • Account Information: Username, password, preferences.
  • Transaction Data: Purchase history, order details, payment records.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device info.
  • Cookies: Tracking data to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders.
  • Communicate about products, services, and offers.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Improve website/app experience.
  • Secure transactions and prevent fraud.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (payment gateways, delivery partners, IT support).
  • Legal authorities when required by law.
  • Business transfers (mergers, acquisitions, sales).

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and personalize ads. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We apply industry-standard security practices to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or update your data.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
